About Royal Cedar

Royal Cedar is a boutique hotel nestled in the heart of Shimla, perched among snow-kissed cedar trees. We blend classic mountain charm with contemporary comforts, offering bonfire nights, gourmet dining, and personalized service tailored to families and couples alike.

  • • Intimate bonfire evenings with curated drinks
  • • Gourmet seasonal menu featuring local produce
  • • Personalized concierge & winter activity planning
